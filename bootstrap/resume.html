About Me
Who Am I?

tech enthusiast coding = arts self-learner enterpreneur backpack traveller

Hi, thanks for checking out my website! This is a rather personal part, so please read on if you want to get to know more about me as a person! Thanks! :)

First, it's not surprising that I'm a tech enthusiast...

My first program was written in 2009 using Pascal.

I remember distinctly the excitement that rocked my mind when I ran the program: IT. WAS. AWESOME.

And you know what, I was a very simple program that allowed the user to enter 2 integers, and spit out their sum.

But that initial moment inspired me a lot to create more products and write better programs.

And I understand that coding = arts :)

I think there're many ways to write a program, some are better than others.

I have always trained myself to become a programmer who doesn't only write programs that work, but also programs that are clearly-structured, and understanble. I achieve this goal by using documentation for my programs, and writing comments when necessary.

One side note, I'm a big fan of colorful things. That's why I like coloring my programs with text editors (e.g. Sublime Text) or add-ons on top of the original IDEs (e.g. Eclipse). That's why when I look at a beautifully written program, I think it looks like a masterpiece, and that coding = arts. :)

Please check out my GitHub profile to see my sample codes! Thank you!

Best thing about me: I'm a self-learner!

Computer Science is huge, and there's no way my professors can teach me everything, so I motivate myself to take online courses in the fields I'm interested in: Apps Development, Software Development, Artficial Intelligence, and Machine Learning.

You can have a look at the courses that I've taken in the "Courses" tab. Thank you!

And I'm also a enterpreneur!

I want to use technology to narrow the gap between the rich and the poor.

The motivation comes within myself: I didn't have good education until high school, and I understand the advantages of having access to good online courses.

We're living in a world where knowledge is power, and I want to do something to improve people's learning experience with online courses, especially on programming topics.

Lastly, I'm a backpack traveller *woohoo*

I love travelling. I love seeing new people, experiencing new cultures, and visiting new places.

Travelling is an eye-opening experience, because curiosity and the thirst for discovery are in my blood. :)
